Features

Small app. Big perspective.

Everything World Progress does serves one idea: making the passage of time visible.

📊

Four scales of time

Today, this week, this month, and this year — live percentages that update every single second while you watch.

🔄

Flip to countdown

One tap turns “50.61% of 2026 has passed” into “180d 7h left until 2027.” Same truth, two feelings.

🏠

Widgets that never sleep

A bar and a ring for your home screen. They compute time natively on-device — accurate even if you never open the app.

🔔

Milestone alerts

A nudge at 25%, 50%, 75%, 90% and 99% of the year — and a celebration at midnight on New Year's Eve. 🎉

🎨

Make it yours

Six accent colors that restyle the app and both widgets, live, with options like showing the clock on the bar widget.

⏳

Your own countdowns

A birthday, an exam, a trip — add any date and watch its bar fill as the day approaches. Stored on your device, nowhere else.
