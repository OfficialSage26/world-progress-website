03What it does

Four clocks, reframed. Today, this week, this month, and the year — each as a percentage that moves while you watch. Tap once and every number flips from what's gone to what's left.

Widgets that don't lie. Most widgets show you the past — a number from the last time their app happened to run. These compute time natively, on-device, every time they draw. Close the app forever; the widgets stay correct. The bar's clock ticks every minute for free.

Milestones, delivered. A quiet notification at each quarter mark of the year, and one at midnight, December 31.

Yours. Six accent colors. Your own countdowns — an exam, a flight, a birthday. Everything stays on your phone: no account, no tracking, no network permission used. It can't phone home. There's no home to phone.
